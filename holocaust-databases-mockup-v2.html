Holocaust Databases

A unique and critically valuable collection of databases containing information about Holocaust victims and survivors.

As of May 2025, more than 6 million records are available in this continually updated collection.
This is an ongoing project and we welcome additional volunteers.

Search Holocaust Records

Search across all 6 million+ individual records in all 356 component databases.

Search by OR: Use commas to search for either of two names. Example: COHEN,GOLDBERG finds records for either name.

Search by AND: Use a space to require both names. Example: COHEN GOLDBERG finds records containing both.

Exact search includes JewishGen synonyms for town and given names. A search for Lodz will also return Litzmannstadt; a search for Minnie will include Miriam, Mina, Mary, and more.
