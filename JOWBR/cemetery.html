About This Cemetery

Beth David Cemetery, Adolph Ullman Aid Society section, 300 Elmont Road, Elmont, NY, USA. 2,732 burials total.

The cemetery office is at the address given elsewhere in this file. The office phone number is (516) 328-1300.

The Adolph Ullman Aid Society has the largest society plot in the cemetery, being the entire block G8.

The Society also had plots at Mt. Zion in Beth Page, NY, Mt Hebron Cemetery in Kew Gardens Hills, NY, and Wellwood Cemetery in West Babylon, NY.

The Society ceased operation in the first decade of the 21st Century and all records (such as they are) are now in Record Group 2242 at the YIVO Archive in New York City.
