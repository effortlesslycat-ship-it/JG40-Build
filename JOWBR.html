What Is JOWBR?

The JewishGen Online Worldwide Burial Registry is the world's largest centralized database of Jewish burial records — a permanent digital archive of headstone inscriptions, cemetery registers, and historical burial data from every corner of the globe. Records are freely searchable by anyone and updated twice annually.

A Permanent Archive

Cemeteries fall into disrepair. Landsmanshaft societies dissolve. JOWBR ensures records survive — a virtual resting place even when the physical one is lost.

Unique Genealogical Data

Headstones preserve Hebrew names, fathers' names, and death dates unavailable anywhere else — essential for tracing ancestry back to the old country.

One Central Search

Instead of contacting individual cemeteries worldwide, researchers and families can find burial sites in one place — across 144 countries and counting.

Volunteer-Powered

Every record was contributed by a volunteer: photographed, transcribed, and translated. You can help grow the registry for future generations.

Headstone Photos

Over 908,000 gravestone photographs are linked directly to burial records. Can't read Hebrew? JewishGen's ViewMate community can help with translation.

A Mitzvah

Preserving burial records honors the deceased and comforts the living. Hebrew dates of death support yahrzeit observance for families worldwide.
